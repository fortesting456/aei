About us

Aeonianedge takes pride in its experienced and multi-disciplinary teams, as well as its commitment to producing high-quality solutions that are implemented securely, on time, and within budget using in-house resources. We have the specialised plant and professional people to provide both solo services and fully integrated solutions.

Our purpose

Our mission is to ensure reliability of communication and information systems infrastructure through sustainable solutions.

Our
values

People

People are at the heart of our business, which is why we invest substantially in training and development to ensure that our employees feel appreciated and positive. Each of our 1,800 employees across the country contributes significantly to our success.

Relationships

There may be tens of thousands of people in the sector, but we understand that each one is important. So does our reputation. We communicate freely with our clients and stakeholders in order to establish long-term, valued relationships and gain their trust in our ability to produce results.

Innovation

The world is changing at a rapid pace, and our investment and commitment to innovation reflects this. We are one of the few Tier 2 innovators making a genuine difference, thanks to our strong engagement with academia and manufacturing partners to promote efficient, safer, and more sustainable delivery of our projects and help move construction into the twenty-first century.

Can-do attitude.

Many of our projects are set in complicated environments, and we never underestimate the training and experience required to achieve our obligations. With over 40 years of experience behind us, we've developed a strong willingness to tackle any issues that arise and approach each assignment with perseverance and goodwill. Our accomplishments reflect the strict standards that guide everything we do.

Health, Safety, and Wellbeing

At Aeonianedge, health, safety, and well-being are central to all we do, thanks to our extensive training, investment in innovative technologies to lessen the risks of on-site working, and our own occupational health practice for all employees. We even have a Young Safety Leadership Team to ensure that this good practice is sustained for many years to come.
